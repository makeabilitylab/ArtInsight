The artwork features a vibrant fish positioned centrally on a black background. The fish is outlined in black and filled with various colors. Its body is predominantly green with horizontal blue stripes and a yellow tail. The head of the fish displays a mix of colors, including red and purple, with distinct black markings that resemble eyes and gills.

Surrounding the fish are numerous rectangular pieces of colored foam in various shades. These pieces are scattered across the black background and include bright colors such as orange, yellow, blue, and purple. The foam pieces vary in size and orientation, creating a dynamic and playful composition.

The overall layout emphasizes the fish as the focal point, while the colorful foam pieces add texture and contrast against the dark background. The combination of colors and shapes contributes to a lively and engaging visual effect.
